Why Indoor Air Quality Matters?

Indoor Air Quality

90% of our lives spent indoors

2–5x more pollution indoors than outdoors

Reasons:

  • Most indoor spaces especially at Home, Offices and Educational institutes lack real-time feedback about air quality.
  • Humans cannot see harmful gases like CO₂, TVOCs, or fine dust (PM2.5, PM10), so they cannot act until they know the air quality.

3 Things You Must Know

  • 2 in 5 people suffer from allergies caused by these substances.
  • We consume nearly 8 times more air than food.
  • Modern buildings often do not allow enough fresh air to circulate.

WHAT IS INDOOR AIR QUALITY MONITORING SYSTEM ?

Indoor Air Quality Monitoring System (IAQMS) is a smart device that measures and monitors the quality of air in indoor environments. It provides real-time data on various air pollutants, including particulate matter (PM2.5, PM10), Total volatile organic compounds (TVOCs), Carbon dioxide (CO2), Temperature, and Relative humidity . The IAQMS helps individuals and organizations assess and improve indoor air quality, ensuring a healthier and safer environment.
