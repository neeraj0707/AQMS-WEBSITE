Air Quality measurement

Air quality measurement involves monitoring pollutants like PM2.5, PM10, NO2, O3, and CO to assess the safety of the air we breathe. Sensors collect real-time data, which is processed to calculate the Air Quality Index (AQI). This index helps categorize air quality into levels such as Good, Moderate, or Hazardous. Accurate measurement ensures timely updates for health and safety awareness.
